CSDA’s Background, Milestones, and Aspirations




The Cordillera School of Digital Arts, Inc. or CSDA was incorporated and has been duly registered with the Philippine Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) in Baguio City back in May, 2007. The company has since begun to operate under the guidance and regulatory policies of TESDA (Technical Education and Skills Development Authority) and hand-in-hand with the organizational programs of the Animation Council of the Philippines (ACPI).

TESDA REGISTERED


As a Training Center...

CSDA is registered under TESDA (Technical Education and Skills Development Authority), a government agency tasked to manage and supervise technical education and skills in the Philippines as one of the skills training centers in the country. Training in Animation NCII, 2D Digital Animation NCIII, Creative Web Design, and Web Development are facilitated in the school which aims to aid Filipinos to be equipped in landing a job in the growing ICT (Information and Communications Technology) Sector.

As a TESDA Assessment Center...

CSDA is registered under TESDA (Technical Education and Skills Development Authority) as Assessment Center that helps determine graduate of TESDA Training Centers specifically in the field of Animation NCII, 2D Animation NCIII, and Visual Graphics NCIII if the graduate of these programs or worker can perform to the standards expected in the workplace based on the defined competency standards. Certification is provided to those meets the competency standards.
