This program aims to provide students with skills needed to succeed in the field of Animation. The students are guided to take classes in Basic Animation, Storyboarding, Character Design, Background Design, 3D Modelling, and Action Scripting for Animation. For Japanese art enthusiasts, we also added Anime and Manga Comics drawing classes. While for students who seek to upgrade skills in Cartooning, we also have program for Simple, Regular and Realistic Cartooning.


Programs:

  • Basic Drawing and Illustration
  • Nature's Landscape
  • Cityscape
  • Making Faces
  • The Human Figure
  • The Animal Kingdom
  • Character Creation
  • Storyboarding
  • Basic Flash Animation
  • Advanced Flash Animation

Here at CSDA, we always have fun creating ideas!
