CSDA is registered under TESDA (Technical Education and Skills Development Authority), a government agency tasked to manage and supervise technical education and skills in
the Philippines as one of the skills training centers in the country. Training in Animation NCII, 2D Digital Animation NCIII, Creative Web Design, and Web Development are facilitated
in the school which aims to aid Filipinos to be equipped in landing a job in the growing ICT (Information and Communications Technology) Sector.

Know More

CSDA offers a wide range of modular programs in Creative Media Arts, Animation & Design, and Game Development. This program is offered to people who are keen on
developing skills in the different areas of Digital Arts and Game Development. This program aims to help students master different skills and build portfolio that could help them
to move forward in a career in the creative media and game development industry.

CSDA's Summer Arts & Animation Workshop or SAAW is a program that is crafted for those who wants to make their summer breaks productive through learning at the same time
having fun with creating art and/or animation projects.
