Nominal Duration : 108 hours

Course Description :
The Web Development Program is designed to train individuals from being the absolute newbie to the confident real-world web developer using open-source technologies – Apache, MySQL, and Php.

The modules are designed to be project-based to better enable individuals to get hands-on experience in developing a progressive website. A live domain will be used in the entire program so that students would have a better appreciation of how their site develops in the world-wide-web.

In addition to the progressive project, individuals who undertake the development program will be subjected to an online examination that will be administered at the end of the 108-hour course.

Trainee Entry Requirements:

  • Must be at least HS graduate
  • Must have a basic understanding of World Wide Web
  • Must have basic knowledge in computer operations
